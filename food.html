TASTE 

  Some would argue Taste serves

  the very best coffee in all of St. Andrews.

  Its proximity to the library and late opening hours

  means it’s perfect for that late night pick me up.

  The atmosphere means this wee cafe is lovely to study in

  — if you can get a seat that is!

NARDINI 

 
  Who doesn't love good Italian coffee

  and if that comes with a delicious

  cake on a side - yes please!

  Located on South Street,

  Nardini is the best place for

  nice afternoon coffee and cake.

TANON 

 
  One of the rare 'cheap' eats in St Andrews

  makes this casual restaurant a place where seats

  are few and far between. If you're lucky enough

  to find a perch, you'll be treated to some soul-warming

  Thai street food in a cosy setting.

  If not, don't worry as it tastes just as good at home.

CombiniCo. 

 

  CombiniCo is one of the nicest places in St. Andrews

  to grab a quick lunch. Not only is the food in this establishment

  super healthy and tasty, but the aesthetically pleasing design

  of this restaurant makes it a real favourite.

  A definite plus is that they sell MUJI pens!
